## How to Read This File

- **Status**: `open` (being monitored) | `triggered` (convert to action item immediately) | `resolved` | `accepted` (known risk, no action)
- **Close by**: All risks must be reviewed and resolved/accepted within 30 days of opening
- Triggered risks become action items in `execution/action-items.md` — do not leave them as risk entries
