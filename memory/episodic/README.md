# memory/episodic/

Timestamped experience + reasoning records. Layer 1 memory — fast decay.

**What goes here:** Records of what happened AND why you did it that way, what you were uncertain about, and what resulted. Not a log of events — a record of cognitive states.

**What does NOT go here:** Raw notes (use /capture → notes/raw/), knowledge (use /learn → knowledge/), meeting outputs (use /debrief).

**When to create:** After significant decisions, unexpected outcomes, or sessions where the reasoning context would be hard to reconstruct later.

**Skill:** `/mem-capture`
**Schema:** `architecture/MEMORY-SCHEMAS.md` → Schema 1
**File naming:** `ep_YYYYMMDD_NNN.md` (NNN = sequence within the day)
**Review interval:** 14 days from `timestamp`
**Archive trigger:** Unlinked entries older than 90 days → `memory/archive/`
