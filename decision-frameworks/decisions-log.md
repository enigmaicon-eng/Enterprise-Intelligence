# Decisions Log

Append-only. Use template at `templates/decision-entry.md`.

Review dates are tracked here. Every 30 days, scan for entries where `review_date` has passed and update the `outcome` field.

---

## Format

```
---
decision: Short description
date: YYYY-MM-DD
chosen: chosen option
reversibility: high | medium | low | irreversible
review_date: YYYY-MM-DD
outcome: pending | good | acceptable | poor | n/a
---
```
