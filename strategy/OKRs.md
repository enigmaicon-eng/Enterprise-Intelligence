## Design Rules

- **3 objectives maximum.** If everything is a priority, nothing is.
- **3 key results per objective maximum.** More KRs = diluted signal.
- **Every objective traces to an H1 bet.** Phantom objectives (no bet linkage) indicate strategic drift.
- **Every KR is an outcome, not an activity.** "Shipped X" is activity. "X users do Y" is outcome.
- **Signal quality check at every review.** Does this KR actually measure what the objective is pursuing?
