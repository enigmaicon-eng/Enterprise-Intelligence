## How to Read This Log

- **Won**: Bet paid off — thesis confirmed, success signal observed
- **Cut**: Bet closed before winning — kill condition triggered or resources reallocated
- **Time**: Duration from open to close (not horizon classification)
- **Learning**: Single most useful pattern extracted; full detail in postmortem
