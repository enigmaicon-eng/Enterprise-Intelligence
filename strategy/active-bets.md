# Active Bets

Bets are directional commitments of time, attention, or resources where the outcome is uncertain. Each bet has a thesis, a time horizon, and a kill condition.

**Bet portfolio target:** 3-5 active bets at any time. More dilutes attention; fewer concentrates risk.

---

## Template

```
### Bet Name
- **Thesis:** Why this bet is worth making
- **Horizon:** H1 (0-3mo) | H2 (3-12mo) | H3 (12mo+)
- **Investment:** Time/attention/resources committed
- **Success signal:** What would tell you this bet is paying off?
- **Kill condition:** What would tell you to cut this bet?
- **Status:** Active | Paused | Won | Cut
- **Last reviewed:** YYYY-MM-DD
```
